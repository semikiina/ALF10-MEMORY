HOW DOES IT WORKS?


With the power of a microchip directly connected to the user’s brain you can change your day-to-day life. The main feature is the capability of keeping the memories you wish. Let’s be honest: how many times did you saw your keys and 5 minutes later forgot where they were? This ends with ALT+F10. With only the thought you can activate a function on the microchip and and remenber where the keys were. It will upload to an external cloud you’re last 5 minute seen memories
